job specification

data analysist

A data analyst is responsible for collecting, processing, and analyzing large amounts of data in order to identify patterns and trends. They need to have expertise in data analysis tools such as SQL, Excel, and Python, as well as experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI. Other key skills include strong attention to detail, problem-solving ability, and the ability to work independently or as part of a team. Good communication skills and the ability to present data in a clear and concise manner are also important. Experience with statistical analysis and machine learning algorithms is a plus

web developer

A web developer is responsible for creating and maintaining websites. They need to have a strong understanding of coding languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as experience with web development frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue. They should also have experience with database management systems such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB. Other key skills include attention to detail, problem-solving ability, and the ability to work independently or as part of a team. Good communication skills and the ability to work under tight deadlines are also important
